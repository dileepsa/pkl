Welcome To Kabbadi World

Kabaddi is a contact team sport.It was introduced about 4000 years ago in Tamil Nadu, India.Played between two teams of seven players,the objective of the game is for a single player on offence, referred to as a raider to run into the opposing team's half of a court, touch out as many of their defenders as possible, and return to their own half of the court, all without being tackled by the defenders, and in a single breath.

Bengaluru Bulls

Bengaluru Bulls (BGB) is a Kabaddi team based in Bengaluru that plays in the Pro Kabaddi League. The team were champions in Season 6 and are led by Pawan Sehrawat and coached by Randhir Singh. The team is owned by Kosmik Global Media. Bulls play their home matches at the Kanteerava Indoor Stadium.
